# FIFA 23

---

## Game Information

**Game Name:** FIFA 23  
**Platform:** Available on PlayStation and Xbox consoles

---

## Description

FIFA 23 brings even more action and realism to the pitch with Hypermotion2 technology, men's and women's World Cup tournaments, and cross-play features. Experience the world's game like never before with enhanced gameplay, updated rosters, and new ways to play.

Key features include:
- Hypermotion2 technology for realistic player movement
- FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 and FIFA Women's World Cup 2023 tournaments
- Cross-play between same-generation consoles
- Improved set pieces and penalty mechanics
- Updated teams, leagues, and stadiums
