# Enhanced Stitch Prompt: Video Game Store Homepage

## Foundation & Vibe
A minimalist, high-performance, and utilitarian landing page for a local physical video game store. The aesthetic is clean, focused, and fast-loading, avoiding complex animations or heavy graphics.

## Target Audience
Local gamers in Algeria (DZD currency) who need quick, real-time information before visiting the store.

## Screen: Homepage
### Hero Section
- **Branding:** Clear, bold typography for "Video Game Store".
- **Welcome Message:** "Your local destination for console gaming. Check availability and browse our catalog before you visit."
- **Primary CTAs:** Two large, high-contrast, touch-friendly buttons side-by-side or stacked on mobile:
    1. **"View Consoles"** (Primary style, solid background)
    2. **"Browse Games"** (Secondary style, outlined)

### "Why Visit Us?" Section
A clean grid or list with 4 key value propositions, each paired with a minimalist Lucide icon:
1. **Real-time Status:** See if a console is Available or Reserved right now.
2. **Full Game Library:** Browse every game installed on our PS5, Xbox, and Switch consoles.
3. **Transparent Pricing:** Know our rates (starting from 50 DZD) before you arrive.
4. **No Reservations Needed:** Just walk in and play.

### Footer
- **Call to Action:** "Visit us today — walk-ins welcome!"
- **Style:** Minimalist, low-contrast text to keep focus on the primary buttons.

## Visual Style & Technical Constraints
- **Color Palette:** High-contrast (e.g., Deep charcoal/black backgrounds with sharp white/electric blue accents).
- **Typography:** Refined, characterful sans-serif (e.g., a modern geometric font).
- **Buttons:** 2px solid borders, sharp or slightly rounded corners (8px). Touch targets must be at least 48px.
- **Performance:** No heavy background images. Use CSS patterns or subtle noise textures if needed for depth.
- **Language:** English (with DZD currency references).
